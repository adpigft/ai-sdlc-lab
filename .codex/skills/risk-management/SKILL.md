# Risk Management Skill

## Purpose
Manage delivery, architecture, security, operational, and compliance risks.

## When to use
Use when a risk register or mitigation plan is needed.

## Inputs
- risks
- architecture artifacts
- implementation plan
- release plan

## Process
1. Identify and classify risks.
2. Assess likelihood and impact.
3. Define mitigations and owners.
4. Return a risk register.

## Outputs
- risk register

## Quality checks
- risk classification
- owner assignment
- mitigation clarity
- residual risk visibility

## Human gate
Human approval is required for risk acceptance.
