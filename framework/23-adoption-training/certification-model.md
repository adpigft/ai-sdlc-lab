# Certification Model

## Purpose

This model defines future certification levels for AI-SDLC adoption.

## Certification Levels

- AI-SDLC Associate
- AI-SDLC Practitioner
- AI-SDLC Architect
- AI-SDLC Enterprise Lead

## Notes

- Certifications should be evidence-based and role-appropriate.
- Certification does not replace accountability or approval authority.
