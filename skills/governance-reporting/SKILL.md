# Governance Reporting Skill

## Purpose
Create management reporting for delivery status, AI adoption, quality, risks, blockers, and KPI trends.

## When to use
Use when governance summaries and trend reporting are needed.

## Inputs
- delivery status
- AI adoption data
- quality metrics
- risks
- blockers
- KPI trends

## Process
1. Gather governance signals.
2. Summarize status and trends.
3. Highlight risks and blockers.
4. Return a governance summary.

## Outputs
- governance summary

## Quality checks
- status accuracy
- trend clarity
- risk visibility
- blocker visibility

## Human gate
Human review is required before external distribution.
